      <h1>Time Management</h1>
      <span class="version-badge">Version 1.2.2</span>
      
      <p>
         <strong>Time Management</strong> is a native time-tracking and productivity application built exclusively for Ubuntu Touch phones.
         It empowers users to manage projects, tasks, timesheets, and activities‚Äîboth offline and online‚Äîthrough seamless integration with CURQ and Odoo servers.
      </p>

      <h2>What Does This App Do?</h2>
      <p>
         This app is your all-in-one productivity companion on Ubuntu Touch. Whether you're a freelancer tracking billable hours, 
         a project manager overseeing team tasks, or simply someone who wants to stay organized, Time Management has you covered.
      </p>
      
      <h3>Key Features</h3>
      <ul>
         <li><strong>Project Management:</strong> Create and organize projects with full hierarchy support (Projects ‚Üí Subprojects ‚Üí Tasks ‚Üí Subtasks)</li>
         <li><strong>Task Tracking:</strong> Manage tasks with deadlines, priorities, stages, and assignees</li>
         <li><strong>Timesheet Logging:</strong> Track your work hours with built-in timers and sync to your server</li>
         <li><strong>Activity Management:</strong> Log and schedule activities linked to projects and tasks</li>
         <li><strong>Project Updates:</strong> Create and share project status updates with rich text support</li>
         <li><strong>Push Notifications:</strong> Stay informed with real-time notifications for assignments and updates</li>
         <li><strong>Auto-Sync:</strong> Automatic bidirectional synchronization with your CURQ/Odoo server</li>
         <li><strong>Offline Support:</strong> Work offline and sync when connected</li>
         <li><strong>Visual Dashboard:</strong> See where your time goes with charts and analytics</li>
         <li><strong>Multi-Account:</strong> Connect to multiple server instances simultaneously</li>
         <li><strong>Dark Mode:</strong> Full dark theme support for comfortable viewing</li>
      </ul>
